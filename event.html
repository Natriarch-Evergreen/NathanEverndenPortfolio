142nd Brandon Chamber Awards Gala

Graphic Factory was contracted for a printed runway and a photo backdrop for the 142nd Brandon Chamber Awards Gala in May 2025. Four days before the event, I started printing the backdrop, which consisted of a 9ft x 10ft banner. Then came the bigger part of the job, which consisted of 10ft, 40 ft, and 100ft runways. It took almost 2 full days of nonstop printing and pep to complete the printing of that much material.

On the day of the event, I arrived at the Keystone Center in Brandon, Manitoba, to start the installation process. In a team of one, I started with the 100-foot runway first, as the staff from Pinnacle Event Rentals was not finished setting up the other areas when the runways were to be placed. This ended up being the correct decision as the film crew and photographers needed that space later in the day for setup. The floor decal went on smoothly, and I had no trouble with it thanks to the TimberMAXX FLOOR squeegee (this thing was so helpful during the installation process) from Sign Maker Tools.

After the large one was fully installed, I then installed the 40-foot runway in the entrance to the Manitoba room. This was a bit of a challenge due to the small amount of space I had to work with, but it was installed with little to no hiccups throughout the process.

I then installed the 10-foot runway in front of the banner backdrop that was set up by Pinnacle Event Rentals earlier that day, which was a breeze compared to the 100- and 40-foot runways I installed earlier in the day.

Overall it was a large project that looked great during the gala that night. This project took around five hours from when I got to the Keystone Center to when the project was fully installed.
