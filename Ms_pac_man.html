Ms Pac Man Arcade Machine

This custom Ms. Pac-Man arcade cabinet was made for James Chambers, one of the owners of Shea Anglela in Brandon Manitoba, as a Christmas present for his wife, Angie.

He contacted me while still building the cabinet, which gave me time to measure and prepare a template before the time came to install the wrap. The design process involved close collaboration, with four rounds of revisions before James felt it fully captured the retro charm he was aiming for. He had a clear vision of the arcade cabinet from the start, and together we refined the design until he was happy.

Using Graphic Factory it was printed on Controletac 40c-10r, laminated with Scotchcal 8518, and installed by yours truly. At first, I was a bit nervous as it was my first time installing vinyl on a textured wooden surface, but everything went on without a hitch, James and Angie were happy with the result, and it was a wonderful surprise for Christmas morning.

James uploaded the blueprints for the arcade cabinet here if you ever want to build your own. If you are looking for the wrap template, feel free to contact me, and I will send it over to you.
