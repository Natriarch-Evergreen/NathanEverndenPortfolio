The Massage Temple

The first project for TMT consisted of the production and installation of their sign and window decals for their storefront on Rosser Ave. in Brandon, Manitoba.

They already had a design ready for me to print. We also had to split the sign in half to accommodate the size of the material that was ordered to the shop. Lucjkly Cardinal Signs was able to manufacture the sign bracket for us to install on quick notice. We also had to remove the previous sign that was installed on the building, but that came down easily enough.

On the installation day, I had Chris from Chris Macaluso Designs help me with the process. It ended up turning out great, and the owners of The Massage Temple were happy with our work.

I also printed stickers for their in-shop use and their store merchandise. I also cut their logo and installed it on some hot/cold gel heating pads for them to sell in their store.

Later I ended up decaling a car window with their branding while it was getting detailed at Resurrection Auto Detailing.

I've had a ton of great projects and learning opportunities regarding client communication during my time working with TMT and would love to work with them in the future if I get the chance.
