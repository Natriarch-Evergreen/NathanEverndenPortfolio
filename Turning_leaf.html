Turning Leaf Brandon

This project consisted of the design, production, and installation of several signage elements for Turning Leafâ€™s new office in Brandon, Manitoba. It included two 8 ft x 3 ft exterior signs, two large interior wall decals, a window decal, and room labels throughout the office.

The first things printed and installed were the wall decals and room labels for the interior of the building. One of the wall decals was placed too high for the client's liking, so I went in and replaced it later with the specifications they wanted.

This was one of my first jobs at Graphic Factory and the communication was happening with my boss and the client at the same time. This ended up causing a miscommunication regarding the window decal, and the wrong decal was placed on the window. We made things right in the end and came back with the correct decal when we were installing the exterior signs.

I had help from Chris from Chris Macaluso Design to install the exterior signs. We had to do it over 2 weeks due to our supplier supplying each sign frame individually, but the people at Turning Leaf were very understanding and allowed us to complete the project that way.

Unfortunately, the day we installed the second sign, it was snowing and -23, but we got it up and secured without losing any fingers from the cold.

Overall, this was a successful project with minor setbacks and great learning opportunities. The people at Turning Leaf were happy with the end result.
