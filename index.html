Welcome to my Website

My name is Nathan Evernden and I'm a graphic designer who has been working in and adjacent to the design industry for the last 4 years. Since graduating from the Digital Art and Design program at Assiniboine Community College I've been working hard to enhance my creativity and fulfill my passions.

I'm currently in charge of a print shop in Brandon Manitoba called Graphic Factory. I have been working as a graphic designer, print shop manager, and installer since July 2024 giving me a broad set of skills allowing me to complete projects that come my way.
