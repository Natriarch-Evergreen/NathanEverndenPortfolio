About Me

My name is Nathan Evernden and I'm a versatile graphic designer who has been working in and adjacent to the design industry for the last 4 years. After graduating from the Digital Art and Design program at Assiniboine Community College I've been working hard to enhance my creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills to fulfill my passions both personally and professionally.

I currently in manage a print shop in Brandon Manitoba called Graphic Factory. Since taking on the role in July 2024, I’ve been fulfilling multiple positions as a graphic designer, print shop manager, and installer. This has helped me develop a broad skill set from designing, and printing to overseeing installations, allowing me to complete all of the projects that come my way.

I was born in Alberta and lived there until moving with my family to Brandon Manitoba where I now reside. I love adventure and make an effort to get out of the city at least once a week to recharge and explore.

Outside of work, some of my favorite ways to unwind are motorcycle camping, mountain biking, game development, and working on personal projects whatever they may be at the time.
